# Privacy Policy for Coffee Break Extension

Effective Date: 2026

Coffee Break is committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect
Coffee Break does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

We do not collect:
- Personal data (name, email, etc.)
- Browsing history
- Location data
- Financial information
- Authentication data

## How the Extension Works
This extension operates entirely on your local device.  
All settings (such as reminder intervals and preferences) are stored locally using Chrome storage.

## Data Sharing
We do NOT sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties.

## Permissions Usage
- Storage: Used to save user settings locally
- Alarms: Used to schedule reminders
- Tabs & Scripting: Used only to display break reminders on active pages

This policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
